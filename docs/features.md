# Features & Capabilities (v0.9.0 Mega Update)

The **Ultimate Image Converter** is designed to be a comprehensive, privacy-first tool for all your image processing needs.

🚨 **Zero-Upload Guarantee:** Every single feature documented below runs directly inside your web browser. Your device's processor does all the work. At no point is a single byte of your photos uploaded to a third-party server.

## 🚀 Core Capabilities

### 1. Robust File Support
*   **Input Formats**: PNG, JPEG, GIF, WEBP, TIFF, **HEIC**, **HEIF**.
*   **Output Formats**: JPEG, PNG, WEBP, and **AVIF** *(New in v0.8.5)*.
*   **HEIC Support**: Automatically converts Apple's High Efficiency Image Format (HEIC/HEIF) to standard web formats directly in the browser.

### 2. Dual Operation Modes

#### Single File Editor
Detailed, granular control for perfecting individual images.
*   **Real-time Preview**: See changes instantly as you adjust sliders.
*   **Compare Slider**: Quickly toggle between original and edited versions with an interactive drag slider. *(New in v0.9.0)*
*   **Interactive Cropping**: Launch a dedicated modal to visually drag and frame your subject perfectly before converting. *(New in v0.8.5)*
*   **Exif Data**: View and choose to preserve or strip sensitive metadata (GPS, Camera settings).

#### Batch Processor
Power through large collections of images efficiently.
*   **Bulk Conversion**: Convert multiple files at once.
*   **Custom Watermarking**: Protect your IP. Batch-stamp a custom text or transparent PNG logo across all your images. *(New in v0.8.5)*
*   **Unified Archives**: Download all processed images in a single `.zip` archive, create a stitched multi-page `.pdf` document, or generate a Comic Book Archive `.cbz` file.
*   **Limits**: Optimized for performance with a 15-file / 200MB batch limit.

## 🛠 Image Transformations

### 1. Auto-Compression & Sizing
*   **Target Size Auto-Compress**: Type your desired max file size (e.g., 200KB) and the app will rapidly binary-search the absolute highest visual quality that fits within your constraint. *(New in v0.8.5)*
*   **Custom Dimensions**: Resize images to specific width/height.
*   **Aspect Ratio Lock**: Maintain the original proportions prevents image distortion.

### 2. Orientation Control
*   **Rotation**: Rotate images left or right in 90-degree increments.
*   **Flipping**: Mirror images horizontally or vertically.

### 3. Adjustment Lab
Fine-tune the look of your images with professional-grade controls:
*   **Contrast**: Increase or decrease the difference between light and dark areas.
*   **Saturation**: Control the intensity of colors.
*   **Hue**: Shift the color spectrum for creative effects.
*   **Vibrance**: Smartly boost muted colors without oversaturating skin tones.

### 4. Artistic Filters
Apply instant style with one-click filters:
*   **Grayscale & Sepia**: Convert to classic black & white or warm vintage tone.
*   **Duotone & Invert**: Create cyberpunk aesthetic matrix coloring or negative photographic inversion. *(New in v0.9.0)*
*   **Pixelate**: Crush image smoothness to scalable mosaic blocks for retro gaming effects. *(New in v0.9.0)*
*   **Vignette & Blur**: Draw the subjects eye into focus with edge darkening or apply a privacy soft focus. *(New in v0.9.0)*

### 5. AI Toolkit (Powered by WASM / ONNX)
*   **AI Super Resolution**: Increase image resolution using local ONNX Neural Networks with 2x and 4x smart upscaling capabilities directly in your browser. *(New in v0.9.0)*
*   **Background Removal**: Deep-learning background segmentation running entirely in your browser. Cut out the subject of any portrait or product photo with a single click. The AI models are fully pre-packaged within the app, removing any requirement to connect to external CDNs. *(New in v0.8.5)*
*   **Gemini Watermark Removal**: Mathematically and losslessly removes the visible ✨ AI-Sparkle watermark from images generated by Google Gemini. Use this to clean up AI generations for presentations or aesthetic mocks.
    *   **Limitations**:
        *   Only removes Gemini visible watermarks (the semi-transparent logo in bottom-right)
        *   Does not remove invisible/steganographic watermarks. (Learn more about SynthID)
        *   Designed for Gemini's current watermark pattern (as of 2025)
    *   *Thanks to Abhin Krishna, author of the [gemini-watermark-remover](https://github.com/dearabhin/gemini-watermark-remover)*

## 🌐 Modern Web Features

### 1. PWA (Progressive Web App) Support
*   **Installable**: Install the app to your desktop or mobile home screen for a native app-like experience.
*   **Offline Capable**: Works completely offline once loaded. No internet connection required for image processing!

### 2. Privacy First
*   **Zero Uploads**: All processing happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly and Canvas APIs. Your photos truly never leave your device.

### 3. User Experience
*   **Clipboard Support (Ctrl+V)**: Paste an image directly from your computer's clipboard into the app for lightning-fast uploads. *(New in v0.9.0)*
*   **Drag-and-Drop Support**: Easily upload files with fully working drag-and-drop functionality across the app.
