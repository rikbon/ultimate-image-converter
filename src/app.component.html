Quick Start Guide

1. Modes of Operation

  • Single File: Best for granular editing, cropping, and visualizing fine-tuned adjustments before downloading.
  • Batch Conversion: Best for converting large folders of images simultaneously. It utilizes web-workers to process files in parallel without freezing your browser.

2. Core Features

  • Format Support: HEIC, WebP, AVIF, PNG, JPG, TIFF, and GIF are all fully supported.
  • Clipboard (Ctrl+V): Simply copy an image anywhere on your computer and hit Ctrl+V while looking at this page to instantly load it!
  • Auto-Compress Target: Specific a file size limit (e.g. 250KB) and the app will logically step through qualities until it finds the absolute best appearance that perfectly fits your requested size footprint.

3. AI Toolkit

  • Remove Background: Uses the isnet_fp16 neural network running locally on your hardware to isolate the subject of an image. The first load might take a second as it caches the AI model to your browser.
  • AI Super Resolution (Upscaling): Uses ONNX web to intelligently upscale small images by 2x or 4x, maintaining sharp edges without blurring.
  • Remove Gemini Sparkle: Losslessly calculates and extracts the Google Gemini watermark from raw pixels.

4. Privacy Guarantee

This tool operates entirely on the client-side using WebAssembly (WASM). Your photos are mathematically processed by your local CPU/GPU using the HTML5 Canvas API in your browser's memory. No image is ever transmitted to an external server. Feel free to turn off your Wi-Fi and continue working!
